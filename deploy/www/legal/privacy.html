Privacy Policy

Effective: 2026-07-21 · Version 1.0 (MVP scaffold)

1. Controller

Arcanada operates Disk Arcana (disk.arcanada.ai). Contact: disk@arcanada.ai.

2. Data we collect

  • Account data: email, tenant identifier, authentication metadata (OAuth provider when used), email verification status.
  • Service metadata: registered devices (node ID, display name, platform, last seen), vault identifiers, sync conflict records, storage usage counters.
  • Synced content: file paths and hashes stored for sync; optional end-to-end encryption means Arcanada cannot read ciphertext content when E2EE is enabled on a vault.
  • Technical logs: IP addresses, request timestamps, and error diagnostics retained for security and operations (short retention).
  • Product analytics (optional): if you opt in on the dashboard, anonymous usage events (e.g. onboarding checklist progress) are sent to PostHog. No synced file paths or file contents are included. You can withdraw consent anytime in dashboard settings.

3. Purposes and legal bases (GDPR)

  • Provide the service — contract (Art. 6(1)(b)).
  • Security, abuse prevention — legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f)).
  • Billing where applicable — contract / legal obligation.
  • Marketing only with consent where required.

4. Retention

Account data is kept while your account is active. After deletion, we remove or anonymize personal data within 30 days except where law requires longer retention (e.g. billing records).

5. Your rights

Where GDPR applies you may request access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, and object to processing. Use the dashboard Export my data button or Delete account (email confirmation required) or email disk@arcanada.ai. You may lodge a complaint with your supervisory authority.

6. Processors and transfers

Infrastructure providers (hosting, CDN) act as processors under our instructions. See the sub-processor registry. Data is primarily processed in the EU/EEA. Transfers outside the EEA use appropriate safeguards (SCCs) when required.

7. Changes

We will post material changes on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use after notice constitutes acceptance where permitted by law.
