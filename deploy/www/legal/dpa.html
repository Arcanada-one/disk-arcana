Data Processing Agreement (summary)

Effective: 2026-07-21 · Version 1.0 (MVP scaffold)

This summary describes how Arcanada processes personal data on behalf of business customers using Disk Arcana cloud. A signed enterprise DPA with Standard Contractual Clauses is available on request.

Roles

Customer is the data controller for content synced through their tenant. Arcanada is the processor for account and sync metadata required to operate the service.

Subject matter and duration

Processing covers file sync, device enrollment, conflict resolution metadata, and account administration for the subscription term.

Processor obligations

  • Process only on documented instructions (these terms + customer configuration).
  • Ensure confidentiality of personnel with access.
  • Implement appropriate technical and organizational measures (encryption in transit, tenant isolation, access controls).
  • Assist with data subject requests via the export API and support channel.
  • Notify customers of personal data breaches without undue delay.
  • Delete or return data after termination per the Privacy Policy retention schedule.

Sub-processors

A current list of infrastructure sub-processors is available on request. Customers will be notified of material changes with an opportunity to object where required by law.

Contact

DPA requests: disk@arcanada.ai. See also Privacy Policy.
